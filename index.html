About Me

Malaz Tamim is currently a Master's Student in Data Engineering and Analytics with a concentration in Computer Graphics and Vision at Technical University of Munich (TUM), fully funded by DAAD. He graduated from the American University of Beirut in June 2022 with a B.S. in Computer Science, fully funded by the The U.S.-Middle East Partnership Initiative (MEPI) through the Tomorrows's Leaders Program. He worked as a machine learning researcher at the American University of Beirut and as a Data Scientist at Infineon Technogies. His research interests are machine learning and deep learning applications in computer vision.
